FAQs

How is my mood impacted by what I watch?

When we look at the three most common genres that users watch, Comedy, Romance, and Horror, it is easy to see why the mood and media are so connected. Comedies and romance-based media introduce an accessible source of laughter that offers much-needed distance from personal problems whilst enhancing a sense of well-being, In fact, this type of content can decrease stress hormones. Horror movies can replicate the excitement felt by activities like sky-diving by causing spikes in adrenaline and cortisol levels. The anticipation experienced whilst watching this type of content manifests in excitement and can divert anxious thoughts.
